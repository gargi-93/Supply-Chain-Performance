# Supply Chain Performance Analysis

This project analyzes end-to-end supply chain performance using operational, logistics, and supplier data to identify cost drivers, efficiency gaps, and risk areas.

The dashboard focuses on revenue, profit, inventory stockout risk, supplier reliability, and logistics efficiency using Power BI and DAX-based metrics.

## Project Objective
To evaluate supply chain performance and support data-driven decisions by analyzing:
- Revenue, cost, and profit 
- Inventory stockout risks
- Supplier performance and risk
- Logistics carrier efficiency

## Tools & Technologies
- **Power BI** – dashboard design and analytics
- **Power Query** – data cleaning and modeling
- **DAX** – calculated measures and composite scores
- **CSV dataset** – supply chain operational data

## Key Features
- Executive-level KPI overview
- Inventory stockout risk analysis
- Supplier risk scoring using lead time and defect rates
- Logistics carrier efficiency scoring
- Interactive filtering by location and product type

## Key Insights
- Certain products show high stockout risk due to demand exceeding inventory
- Supplier performance varies significantly across defect rates and lead times
- Logistics carriers differ in efficiency when balancing cost, time, and volume
